Split Adventure

May 7–10, 2026
3 nights · 4 days
Old Town · Marjan · Bačvice

Three days of ancient Roman architecture, Adriatic coastline, and some of Croatia's best nightlife — all within walking distance of each other.

Money
  • Combo tickets for Cathedral attractions
  • Palace areas largely free to explore
  • Beach bars cheaper than Old Town
  • Charlie's: great value liter cocktails
Architecture
  • Diocletian's Palace: UNESCO, still lived in
  • Bell tower has the best views in town
  • Before 10 am to avoid crowds
  • Built 305 AD — oldest inhabited palace
Nightlife
  • Charlie's Bar: affordable, great vibe
  • Boiler Club: check DJ lineup first
  • Split After Dark: boat party, highly recommended
  • Everything walkable — no taxis needed
