Split Adventure

May 7-10, 2026 • Croatia 🇭🇷

Your Perfect Split Experience

Get ready for an unforgettable Croatian adventure mixing ancient Roman architecture, stunning coastal views, and amazing nightlife! This itinerary balances history, nature, beach time, and party vibes.

💰 Money Tips

  • Buy combo tickets for Cathedral attractions
  • Many palace areas are FREE to explore
  • Beach bars cheaper than Old Town
  • Charlie's Bar has great liter cocktails

🏛️ Architecture

  • Diocletian's Palace is a living UNESCO site
  • Cathedral bell tower has best views
  • Arrive early (before 10am) to avoid crowds
  • Palace built in 305 AD - still lived in!

🎉 Nightlife

  • Charlie's Bar: affordable, great vibe
  • Boiler Club: upscale, check DJ lineup
  • Split After Dark: boat party (highly recommended!)
  • Everything walkable - no taxis needed
Day 1 • Wednesday, May 7

Ancient Split & First Night Out

Start with the crown jewel - Diocletian's Palace, then ease into Split's nightlife scene

9:00 AM

Diocletian's Palace

⏱️ 2 hours

Arrive early morning (before 10am) to explore without crowds. This 1700-year-old Roman palace is the heart of Split's old town - you'll be walking through living history!

11:30 AM

Saint Domnius Cathedral

⏱️ 1 hour

Climb the bell tower for panoramic views of Split - the stairs are steep but totally worth it. This is the world's oldest Catholic cathedral still in its original structure!

1:00 PM

Gregory of Nin Statue

⏱️ 15 minutes

Don't forget the tradition - rub the big toe for good luck! The toe is super shiny from all the wishes.

2:00 PM

Riva Promenade

⏱️ 2 hours

The famous Riva promenade - perfect for lunch at one of the cafes and people-watching. This is Split's living room!

9:00 PM

Charlie's Bar Split

⏱️ 3 hours

Start your night here! Affordable drinks, great atmosphere, and famous frozen cocktails. Very popular with young crowds.

Day 2 • Thursday, May 8

Nature & Views Day

Escape to Marjan Hill for incredible views, then hit the beach before experiencing Split's club scene

9:00 AM

Marjan Hill

⏱️ 3 hours

The 'lungs of the city' - beautiful hiking paths with stunning viewpoints. Bring water and wear comfortable shoes!

1:00 PM

Meštrović Gallery

⏱️ 1.5 hours

Beautiful sculptures in peaceful gardens with Adriatic views. Check if the main building is open (sometimes under renovation).

3:30 PM

Bačvice Beach

⏱️ 3 hours

Sandy beach perfect for swimming! Shallow waters, bars nearby. Great spot to relax before tonight's party.

8:00 PM

Split Rooftop Bar

⏱️ 1.5 hours

Pre-club drinks with stunning sunset views over old town. Pricey (€9-20 cocktails) but the view is incredible!

11:00 PM

Boiler Club

⏱️ 4 hours

High-end club with great DJs and unique live viola performances. €30 entry but worth it for the right night - check their lineup!

Day 3 • Friday, May 9

Final Exploration & Party

Flexible day for anything you missed, shopping, or a boat party to cap off your trip!

11:00 AM

Diocletian's Palace (Revisit)

⏱️ 3 hours

Revisit for daytime shopping in the narrow streets - great souvenirs and local products. Grab lunch in one of the konobas (taverns).

8:30 PM

Split After Dark (Boat Party!)

⏱️ 5+ hours

Epic boat party experience! Sunset cruise with DJ, drinks, and amazing vibes. This is THE experience for groups of friends in Split. 🎉⛵
